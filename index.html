Kia ora Taiao - Hello World!
Welcome to my reflections blog, where I document my journey towards becoming a software developer.

This is a new chapter of my life - I am officially a broke student again, pursuing a career change and embracing the challenge of learning how to code and becoming a programmer.

Since finishing my undergrad, I've procrastinated figuring out what I truly wanted as a career. The problem is that there are too many fields I would love to get into and I can't seem to settle on just one. Fortunately, today's world is tech-dominated and programming skills open up many doors. Since I've always enjoyed puzzle-solving, I decided to give software development a shot. My goal is to get involved with all the different fields that have been of interest to me and contribute as a developer.

My blog is divided up into two sections - Tech and Human Skills.

There are two sides to being a good programmer - developing technical coding skills is just as important as growth in learning how to problem-solve well, fostering good habits around focus and time management, and working well with other people. This includes learning how to reflect, communicate, collaborate, and appreciate diverse perspectives.

Follow along my journey and keep an eye on this page for weekly updates!
